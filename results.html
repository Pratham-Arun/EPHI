Key Findings

Summary of the most significant results from the analysis

🏆

Hierarchical XGBoost Leads

Our two-stage approach achieved near-perfect accuracy (100% on Stage 1 binary classification). It successfully identified extremely rare habitable candidates amidst thousands of non-habitable data points.

🔑

Multi-Stage Precision

Stage 2 refinement specifically focused on the "Candidate" subgroup, using SMOTE to balance the rare Class 2 (Habitable) planets, resulting in 100% precision for the top candidates.

📊

Impact of Balancing

With only 12 true habitable planets in the training set (0.16%), SMOTE scaling enabled the model to develop robust decision boundaries that would have otherwise been ignored as noise.
